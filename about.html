What is OpenClaw Skills?

OpenClaw Skills is a curated collection of [tools/skills] designed for developers and AI enthusiasts. Unlike automated aggregators, every listing is manually reviewed for quality, functionality, and relevance.

We started OpenClaw Skills because finding quality AI tools was frustrating. Most directories are either:

  • Automated scrapers with no quality control
  • Pay-to-play platforms where the best listing = highest bidder
  • Abandoned projects with outdated information

We wanted something better: a hand-curated resource that developers can actually trust.

How We Curate

Every tool in our directory goes through a manual review process:

Criteria What We Check
Functionality Does it work? Is it accessible? Does it do what it claims?
Relevance Is it actually useful for our target audience?
Maintenance Is it actively maintained? Last update within 6 months?
Documentation Can someone actually figure out how to use it?
Uniqueness Does it offer something not already covered?
